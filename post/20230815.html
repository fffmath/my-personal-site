E-what

  • fffmath
  • 2023-8-15

The Zhongwu Chisha (What to Eat for Lunch) band, also known as Eat-What band or EW for short, is a band comprised of 22 nd-grade students. We have a diverse selection of songs and our music style is still evolving. Our goal is to bring joy to everyone through music (and possibly other means). Initially, we named ourselves after the Beijing roast duck, but now our answer to the question of what to eat for lunch is: why not try a 驴肉火烧?
